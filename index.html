Freelancer Vagabond

You stumble upon a remote freelancer reveling in curiosity, creativity, and collaboration...

As you approach, you realize the freelancer is indulging in delightful and delicious honeypots...

Snacking appears to be an understatement with this creature...

Hello! I'm Michael and I'm happy you made it here with me! I help creative bootstrappers and teams take their projects to the next level. As a self-employed collaborator, I get joy in working with other solopreneurs, freelancers, nonprofits, and hyperlocal small businesses.

My professional background has always thrived for quality of work, meaningful relationships, and personalized variety. When conventional jobs didn't bridge these gaps, I specialized to diversify not only for skills and experience, but for joy and fulfillment in serving others.

Then, a few years ago, I realized the only way for me to - provide the quality of work I thrived to deliver, and serve unbarred as a positive impact to individuals and teams - was to pursue working with others on my own.

Things I love: adventure traveling, martial arts, reading, and Dungeons & Dragons.

*** In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, my services have expanded! Many of my recurring clients have found value and convenience in adding my other areas of expertise and skills to our time working together. Since expanding my services, it has been positive and exciting to serve new clients with more of what I can do for them! ***

Contact me at the bottom of this page for hiring and service inquiries! I look forward to serving you and your team!
